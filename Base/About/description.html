[h3] Update of the original with some customisation [/h3] The Monstergirls have come to join your colony. They are: The cowgirls, who make nice milk. The baphomets, strong and solitary but caring once tamed. The foxgirls, who are sneaky. The pack animals, centaurs and dryads. The black harpies and red harpies, who produce beautful plumage. The ice fairies and forest fairies, who produce weird dust. The slimegirls, who make red potions. The strange creature, the Impmother. The thrumbogirls and the dragongirls, who are strong fighters. All have advanced intelligence except slimegirls. They are good to keep as pets. [b] What changed from previous version? [/b][list] [*] Baphomets from the original mod were reintroduced [*] Option to remove the custom milks definitions has been removed, It wasn't working anyway (you can still set the mod to use vanilla milk) [*] Monsters were nerfed to be closer to vanilla animals (Production amount/days was diminished, custom milk nutrition was nerfed a lot, and slime sharp armor was reduced) [*] Added some Odyssey abilities and crossbreed (Harpy with Black Harpy and Ice Fairy with Forest Fairy) [*] Settings menu was fixed, now values are properly applied on load [/list] [b] Loading order: Be warned, I have not tested any of the optional patch to other mods[/b][list] [*] CORE [*] A Dog Said (optional) [*] LBE's A Dog Said Easy Patcher (optional) [*] Combat Extended (optional) [*] This mod [/list] Enhanced Patch here: https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3524667457 Update of the mod previously maintained by KiameV, you can check its version for 1.5- : https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2014838309 All credit due to the original author of this mod, Wagon!
